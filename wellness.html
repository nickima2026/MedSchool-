Student Wellness

Medical school is one of the most demanding journeys you will ever take. Your mental health matters just as much as your academic performance. These resources are here whenever you need them — no judgment, no barriers.

Immediate support: 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline · Text HOME to 741741
⚠️

If You Need Help Right Now

Call the Physician Support Line at 1-888-409-0141 (Mon–Fri, 8 AM–12 AM ET). It's free, confidential, staffed by volunteer psychiatrists, and built specifically for medical students and physicians. You can also reach the Crisis Text Line by texting HOME to 741741 any time, day or night.
