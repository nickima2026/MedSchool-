Platform & Tools

Everything you need runs on your Mac Mini

💻

Mac Mini M4

Apple Silicon power with 24GB unified memory. Run everything locally, no cloud dependency.

🤖

Claude Desktop

AI tutor, study partner, and clinical reasoning tool. Context-aware assistance for every course.

📚

Anki Flashcards

Spaced repetition engine with pre-built decks for anatomy, pharmacology, pathology.

🔬

OsiriX MD

Medical imaging viewer. DICOM support for radiology and cross-sectional anatomy.

📖

Markdown Lectures

Textbook-quality content in searchable markdown format with embedded media.

🧬

Interactive Simulations

Pathophysiology simulations, ECG interpretation, pharmacokinetics, and more.

🏥

Clinical Tools

Differential diagnosis generator, dosing calculators, clinical decision trees.

🔍

Full Text Search

Instant search across all lectures, notes, and resources. No lag.
