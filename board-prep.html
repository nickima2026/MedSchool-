Getting Started with Board Prep

Most students begin active board prep during their second year, but starting early with tools like Anki flashcards and Pathoma pays dividends. A strong foundation is built by pairing a primary QBank (UWorld is the gold standard) with a video resource (Boards & Beyond or Pathoma) and daily Anki reviews. Many of these platforms offer free trials or institutional access through Stanford — check with your school before purchasing.
